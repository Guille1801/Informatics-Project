  • Conference of Salas

    26th July 2023 | Posted by ALIANZA LIMA

    Guillermo Salas after winning the Apertura: "This title belongs to everyone, now the Clausura remains". The Alianza Lima coach could not help but shed tears after consecrating himself as the winner of the Apertura Tournament. Alianza Lima established itself as the winner of the Apertura Tournament after beating Binacional 6-1. Guillermo Salas was one of the first to speak after achieving this goal and he couldn't help but shed a few tears over the great moment he is experiencing with the blue and white team. "I thank God, the best start to my career personally, I would never have dreamed of it. It is an important achievement for the institution, but it is still the beginning. The Clausura remains, we have to wait for the Copa Libertadores that leaves us a little mortified", mentioned the technician of Alianza Lima.
