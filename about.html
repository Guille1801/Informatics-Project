About

History

Club Alianza Lima, known as Alianza Lima or simply Alianza, is a sports and social institution based in Lima, Peru. It was founded as a soccer club, on February 15, 1901, by a group of working-class teenagers from the old Chacaritas neighborhood in Lima who wanted to practice this sport. It is currently the longest-running club that plays in the First Division of the Peru. The intimate team has always been labeled as 'the people's team', this because of its great approach to the fans. It is almost impossible not to find a single Alliance supporter in any part of the country. Alianza Lima has always been known for being the team with the beautiful and colorful game, which undoubtedly motivated all the fans to go to the stadium to watch their games. Throughout its history, the blue and white team has had symbolic players of Peruvian football in its ranks. Teófilo Cubillas, Hugo Sotil, Alejandro Villanueva, César Cueto, Pedro León; They are some of the alliance idols who have managed to earn the respect of rival fans. Alianza Lima has shown that it is not only a football club, since it has known how to generate feelings from its fans in tragic moments that it had to experience, such as the crash of its plane in 1987 or the death of Sandro Baylón in 2001; painful moments in which the alliance fan demonstrated his union.

Alianza Lima and the Peruvian soccer team

There are many Alianza players who throughout history have been selected to represent Peru in some international event. From the first South American championships to the five editions that the Peruvian team played World Cups in Uruguay, Mexico, Argentina, Spain and Russia.

Why Alianza Lima?

Alianza Lima is one of the biggest clubs in Peruvian soccer. Throughout its history it has garnered 23 national titles and has brought together great exponents of national football, which has undoubtedly helped it to win the love and sympathy of millions of fans. And it is that the intimate group boasts of being the most popular in the country and its fans are characterized by the great passion they show every time the team plays in Matute or on any field in the interior of the country.
