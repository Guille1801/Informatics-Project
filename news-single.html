Alianza Lima is the winner of "Torneo Apertura" 2023!

26th July 2023 | Posted by ALIANZA LIMA

Alianza Lima was proclaimed champion of the 2023 Apertura Tournament with two days to go and after having given up three points for a WO at the start of the championship. Five specialists analyze the path of intimates to win the first contest of the year and ratify why they are two-time champions.

Everything was in favor for the 'grones' to lift that trophy as they scored enough points for them to have the first option, they thrashed Deportivo Binacional 6-1 at the Alejandro Villanueva stadium with a double from Hernán Barcos and goals 'Aladino', Bryan Reyna, Pablo Lavandeira and Jesús Castillo, so he is halfway to reach the third consecutive national title. The current two-time champion did everything right in the first tournament of the year: he didn't lose at home, he was one of the best visitors and he doesn't have many goals against. Despite the fact that Universitario de Deportes was behind to snatch the tournament from him, he couldn't get it. The 'creams' helped Alianza to celebrate the Apertura in Matute because they couldn't beat UTC and were defeated 1-0 in Cajamarca. That made everything be defined before the last date is played.
